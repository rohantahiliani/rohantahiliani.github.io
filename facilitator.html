My name is Rohan Tahiliani, and I will be the facilitator for this retreat. After going through a deep and transforming spiritual crisis, I was presented with a series of challenges and situations that kept preparing me and building my strength and will. Each time I conquered a challenge, I was faced with something tougher to throw me off. Through obsessive determination and guidance from one of the best and most respected teachers, I finally came to the moment of complete freedom and liberation from all the limits that kept me from the infinite beauty and power of the universe. After first experiencing this state, I realized that this is the state I want to live in every moment.

Overcoming fear, shame, guilt, grief, lies, illusion and attachment, I opened up my my channels and connected with the objective self, higher self, source, universe, spirit or any of the million other names this state has been given. Going deeper into this knowledge, I started rapidly making changes in my life to apply and live by these principles. The work to stay on this path is endless, there are constant challenges and obstacles to fall out of the moment and go back to a dual state. My goal is staying in the moment, to live in a state of disillusionment, to finally achieve the eternal Nirvana.

Pursuing the path of truth and knowledge for multiple lifetimes, I have come to the realization that the only thing that matters is the moment. In the moment, a person can completely change anything and everything about their life. Through many personal experiences, I have found several common obstacles faced by people. I have been assisting and facilitating retreats at a spiritual center in Brazil and I am continuously learning and applying the knowledge that I have channeled over time. I simply offer to guide you and show the obstacles, it is still up to you to choose the changes, and do what is required to be in the moment.

If you have any questions or would like to have a conversation about the retreat or The Infinite 0-1 philosophy and lifestyle, please send an email to be@theinfinite.io.
