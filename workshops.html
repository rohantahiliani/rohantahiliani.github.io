Workshops and Activities

There will be several workshops and activities throughout the day, each day of the retreat. The goal of each workshop is to create a fun and unique environment for each participant to go deeper within themselves and to meet themselves in the moment. Each activity will present a situation which gives you an opportunity to catch yourself doing or thinking something completely different, so you suddenly realize a completely new or very old side of yourself.

Listed below are some workshops, offering is subject to change in any moment.

  • Meet Me in the Moment
  • Where are you Now?
  • How many Me's?
  • Unleash the Monster Within
  • Make Me Real
  • Who is Behind Me?
  • Talk to the Future!
